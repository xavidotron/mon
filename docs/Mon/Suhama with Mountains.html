Suhama with Mountains

Kanji瀕形
Modern kanji瀕形
TransliterationSuama Kata (modernly, "Suhama Kata")
TranslationSandbank Form
Date1470
OwnerSuyama (陶山)
CategoriesGeometric: Suhama, Character: Mountain
NotesKanji and transliteration from Kenmon Shokamon. The kanji description there neglects the mountain (山; "yama") kanji characters below the suhama, a reference to the second kanji in the Suyama family name.

The use of a suhama may also be a pun on the Suyama name.

SourcesKenmon Shokamon (見聞諸家紋; “Various Observed Family Crests”). 1467–1470. http://dl.ndl.go.jp/info:ndljp/pid/2533035. pg. 22.
