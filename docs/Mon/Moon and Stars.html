Moon and Stars

Kanji月星
TransliterationTsuki Hoshi
TranslationMoon and Stars
Date1470
OwnerChiba-no-suke (千葉介), Arao (荒尾; 荒尾), Shuku (宿久)
CategoriesGeometric: Circle (Many Plain), Arrangement: Stars
NotesThe kanji description is present in Kenmon Shokamon.

As in Rotated Three, the "suke" in Chiba-no-suke is a title given to the clan head.

The Sasaki version of Kenmon Shokamon has One and Nine Stars instead of this for the Shuku.

SourcesKenmon Shokamon (見聞諸家紋; “Various Observed Family Crests”). 1467–1470. http://dl.ndl.go.jp/info:ndljp/pid/2533035. pp. 10, 32, 37.
