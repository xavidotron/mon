Sasaki Flowery Seven Treasures

Date1470
CategoriesEnclosure: Seven Treasures, Plant: Flower Square (In Seven Treasures), Arrangement: Charged With (Repeatedly)
NotesA variation on Seven Treasures with Blades with flower rhombuses instead of blades, from the Sasaki version of Kenmon Shokamon.
