Three in Octogon

Kanji隅切り角に三の字
Modern kanji隅切り角に三の字
TransliterationSumi-kiri kaku ni san no ji
TranslationIn a corner-cut square, the character ‘three’
Date1570
OwnerInaba (Ittetsu) Masanari
CategoriesCharacter: Number (Three), Enclosure: Other Geometric
NotesUsed on Inaba (Ittetsu) Masanari's messangers' back flags (sashimono). Enclosure may be a tray ("oshiki", 折敷) used to make religious offerings.
SourcesTurnbull, Stephen. Samurai Heraldry. Osprey Publishing, 2002. pg. 54.
Family Crests of Japan. Stone Bridge Press, 2007. pg. 90.
