Turtle Shell Complex

Date1573
OwnerOta Ieharu
CategoriesPlant: Wood Sorrel, Enclosure: Turtle Shell, Arrangement: In Pale, Character: Number, Character: Other
NotesInside the turtle shell enclosure, there are three distinct charges arranged vertically: wood sorrel, the character two, and the character moon. These last two probably refer to the second month, possibly commemorating a birth or other event.
SourcesAshikagake Bukan (足利家武鑑; “House Ashikaga Book of Heraldry”), Kinkadō (金花堂), 1822. p. 17.
