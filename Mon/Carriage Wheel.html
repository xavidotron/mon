Carriage Wheel

Kanji源氏車
Modern kanji源氏車
TransliterationGenji guruma
TranslationGenji wheel
Date1185
CategoriesWheel
NotesAssociated with the Heian classic, The Tale of Genji, and popular as a motif from the late Heian period. See also Element of the Week: Carriage Wheel at Fireflies Sing.
