Mulberry and One in Ring

Kanji丸に割り梶の葉の内に梶の葉に一つ引き
TransliterationMaru ni Wari Kaji no Ha no Nai ni Kaji no Ha ni Hitotsu-biki
TranslationIn a circle, between cut mulberry leaves, a one pulled [under] a mulberry leaf.
Date1470
OwnerKamiya, Mononobe
CategoriesEnclosure: Ring (Plain, Around Multiple), Plant: Other Leaf, Geometric: Bars (One), Half Charge, Arrangement: In Pale Enclosed
SourcesKenmon Shokamon (見聞諸家紋; “Various Observed Family Crests”). 1467–1470. http://dl.ndl.go.jp/info:ndljp/pid/2533035. pg. 44.
Shoku Kamon de Tadoru Anata no Kakei (続家紋でたどるあなたの家系; “Your Family Lineage Followed with Family Crests, Continued”). Yagishoten (八木書店), 1998. pg. 72. (for Japanese)
