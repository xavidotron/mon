TransliterationKage uchiwa sasa ni ichi no ji
TranslationShadowed, on a bamboo-grass fan, the character "one".
Date1470
Ownera provincial samurai
CategoriesFan, Character, Plant
NotesUsed by a provincial samurai. See also Mon of the Week: Fan with Bamboo on Fireflies Sing.
