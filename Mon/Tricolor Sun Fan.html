Tricolor Sun Fan

Kanji日の丸扇
TransliterationHi no maru ōgi
TranslationCircle of the Sun Fan
Date1614
OwnerSatake Yoshinobu
CategoriesFan, Multicolor
NotesUsed by Satake Yoshinobu in the Battle of Imafuku. Unusually, this version used three colors instead of just two. See also Motif of the Week: Folding Fans at Fireflies Sing.
SourcesTurnbull, Stephen. Samurai Heraldry. Osprey Publishing, 2002. pg. 62.
