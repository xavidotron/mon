Tokugawa Hollyhock

Kanji丸に三つ葵
TransliterationMaru ni mitsu aoi
TranslationIn a circle, three hollyhock [leaves]
Date1567
OwnerTokugawa
CategoriesIn Annulo, Leaf, Circle
NotesThe Tokugawa family crest. The Tokugawa clan ruled Japan in the Edo period. Design supposedly refers to the mythical three-legged crow Yatagarasu. See also Mon of the Week: Hollyhock on Fireflies Sing.
