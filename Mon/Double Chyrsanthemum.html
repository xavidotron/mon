Double Chyrsanthemum

Kanji十六八重菊
TransliterationJyūroku yae kiku
TranslationSixteen-petaled doubled chrysanthemum
Date1333
OwnerVarious emperors
BlazonArgent, a doubled sixteen-petaled chrysanthemum sable.
CategoriesFlower
NotesThis is one of the few mon with restricted usage in period; it was the mon of the Emperor, and was only used as he commanded. Other similar mon are presumptuous for SCA use, as they imply imperial connections: for example, the back of a 14-petaled chrysanthemum was used by imperial princes, and Emperor Go Daigo used a 17-petaled version when exiled. Mon combining the chrysanthemum with other elements are more reasonable, signifying loyalty to the Emperor rather than a connection to him.
