Shadowed, Nine Stars

Kanji陰九曜
TransliterationKage-ku-yō
TranslationShadowed, Nine Stars
Date1160
OwnerEmperor Go-Shirakawa
BlazonArgent, an annulet between eight annulets sable.
CategoriesGeometric
NotesAmong other places, depicted in Heiji Monogatari Emaki on an imperial ox-cart during the Heiji Rebellion.
