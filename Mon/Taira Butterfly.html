Taira Butterfly

Kanji鎧揚羽蝶
TransliterationYoroi agehachō
TranslationArmored swallowtail butterfly
Date1185
OwnerTaira
CategoriesInsect
NotesUsed by the Taira clan, traditionally said to date back to the battle of Dan no Ura in 1185. See also Mon of the Samurai on Fireflies Sing.
