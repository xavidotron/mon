Rotated Three

Kanji號輪皷引領
TransliterationGō Ryūgo Hiki Eri
Translationsymbol a diabolo (juggling top) pulled band
Date1470
OwnerMiura-suke (三浦介)
CategoriesCharacter: Number (Three), Geometric: Bars
NotesThe kanji description comes from the period source. A modern description might be something like「竪三つ引両」(tate mittsu hikiryō; "vertical three pulled amount"). This three-based mon reflects the first character in "Miura". "Suke" (介) here is a title given to the clan head based on their heriditary rule over Sagami Province.
