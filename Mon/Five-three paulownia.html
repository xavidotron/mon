Five-three paulownia

Kanji五三桐
TransliterationGo-san kiri
TranslationFive-three paulownia
Date1392
OwnerAshikaga
BlazonArgent, in pale a paulownia cinquefoil between two paulownia trefoils, all slipped, and a paulownia leaf sable.
CategoriesPaulownia
