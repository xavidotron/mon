Five and Notched Shapes

Date1470
CategoriesGeometric: Other, Character: Number (Other), Geometric: Circle (Many Plain), Arrangement: One-and-Two, Arrangement: Between
NotesThe character here, 𫝀, is a historical variation of 五, five.

The Sasaki version of Kenmon Shokamon has this mon without the small circles or the character five.

SourcesKenmon Shokamon (見聞諸家紋; “Various Observed Family Crests”). 1467–1470. http://dl.ndl.go.jp/info:ndljp/pid/2533035. pg. 36.
